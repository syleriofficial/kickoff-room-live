Between-Match Checklist

After full-time

First 15 Minutes

  1. Give final score reaction and thank chat.
  2. Ask viewers to subscribe for the next match.
  3. End the stream cleanly; do not leave dead air.
  4. Open Shorts Studio and copy the full-time Short package.
  5. Post the first Short before starting the next prep window.
Next setup

30 Minutes Before Next Kickoff

  1. Load the next match preset in Producer Panel.
  2. Reset score, timer, hype, shots, and possession.
  3. Copy the next title/description from Dashboard.
  4. Send Reminder Ops last-call message.
  5. Start private preview, verify audio, then go public.
