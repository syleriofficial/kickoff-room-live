# Community Post Templates

Use these once the Community tab becomes available, or adapt them for Telegram/WhatsApp.

## Pre-Match Poll

```text
Tonight's watchalong: TEAM A vs TEAM B

Who wins?

No footage, no broadcast audio - live score, English reactions, and chat.
```

Poll options:

- TEAM A
- Draw
- TEAM B

## Score Prediction

```text
Drop your score prediction for TEAM A vs TEAM B.

We go live 30 minutes before kickoff with English no-footage watchalong coverage.
```

## First Scorer

```text
First scorer prediction?

Join the live watchalong and we will track chat predictions during the match.
```

## Post-Match

```text
Full-time: TEAM A vs TEAM B

Who was your player of the match?

Shorts and full-time verdict coming next.
```
