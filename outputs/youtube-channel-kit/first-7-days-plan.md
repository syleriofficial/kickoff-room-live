# First 7 Days Plan

## Goal

Build enough activity for YouTube to understand the channel topic before monetization starts.

## Daily Minimum

- 1 scheduled/live watchalong when possible
- 3 Shorts
- 1 community-style post text saved for later
- 1 sponsor/affiliate outreach message

## Day 1

- Complete channel profile, banner, About section, playlists.
- Upload 3 Shorts from templates, even if they are preview-style.
- Schedule first private live once YouTube live is enabled.

## Day 2

- Run first watchalong or test stream.
- Clip 5 Shorts from the stream overlay/reactions.
- Create Telegram/WhatsApp community.

## Day 3

- Improve thumbnails based on click behavior.
- Post 3 Shorts: prediction, key battle, full-time verdict.
- DM 10 small football pages or merch sellers.

## Day 4

- Add affiliate links to descriptions.
- Test auto voice and overlay for 30 minutes.
- Create one tutorial Short: "How to watch with us - no footage."

## Day 5

- Schedule next 2 lives.
- Create a pinned community message.
- DM 10 more sponsor prospects.

## Day 6

- Review analytics:
  - impressions
  - click-through rate
  - average view duration
  - Shorts retention
- Keep the top-performing title style.

## Day 7

- Publish a weekly recap Short.
- Update sponsor pitch with early numbers.
- Prepare next week match presets.

## Simple KPI Targets

- 20 Shorts posted
- 3 live/test streams
- 50 subscribers
- 1,000 total Shorts views
- 20 sponsor DMs sent
